About me

Personal life

I was born in north Georgia and my family moved to eastern Kentucky in August 2007. I live with my mom, dad, and sister who have given me amazing support for everything I do, and Penny has been with us since she was about a year old. I have a huge extended family that takes us on vacation most years. I am currently dorming with my sister since we attend the same college.

Employment

My first job was at Spirit Halloween in 2022, where I would mostly tidy the sales floor but also cashier sometimes. My second job was at Outback Steakhouse in the spring and into summer of 2024, where I greeted people and led them to their tables. Since September, I have worked as a tutor (for five classes!) on-campus at Northern Kentucky University. I am currently the only tutor for ASL 110. I also started working as a Student Wellness Education Ambassador, meaning I work for their tabling events and spread awareness of wellness resources around campus.

Personal interests

Film is my biggest passion. I love watching movies, talking about movies, writing about movies, anything to do with movies. I love horror movies most because of the conversations that surround them and the special effects. I was able to finally decide my college major thanks to a documentary about special effects makeup. My second biggest passion is music, especially George Michael's. I have a dedicated shelf for CDs and cassettes of his albums and singles. I think it was fate that Patience, my favorite album, was released very close to my birthday. His video for "Freeek!" made me sure of my choice of major.
