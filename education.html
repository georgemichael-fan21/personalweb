Education

High school

I graduated from Raceland-Worthington High School with high honors. During my dual-credit English class, I discovered how much I enjoyed writing about horror, and I wrote two high-scoring essays, one comparing the horror and thriller genres and one arguing that horror movies are still scary (even though they do not scare me anymore). I was very certain of what area I wanted to major in so I only formally applied to two colleges: Marshall and Northern Kentucky University. I was awarded merchandise from both of them.

College

I was awarded a Founder's scholarship to attend Northern Kentucky University (I was only 2 ACT points from being eligible for Presidential). I am currently in the middle of my second year, majoring in 3D Digital Design & Visual Effects and minoring in Popular Culture Studies. I was able to write a literature review about misogyny in slasher movies for Media Literacy during my first semester. I am highly considering changing my minor to Theatre since students in the program need credits from working on productions and I originally wanted to study special effects makeup. I believe it will be a better fit as I am also greatly interested in set decoration and costuming.
