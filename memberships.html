Memberships

I became a brother of Sigma Phi Epsilon in fall 2024. I was interested in the volunteering opportunities since I live about two hours away in a small town where nothing much happens. Our main philanthropy event is with St. Baldrick's, a childhood cancer research organization, where several brothers shave their heads in support and we all raise money.

Because volunteering for a certain amount of hours while in SigEp is required, I recently began volunteering with FUEL NKU where I have already made quick work of many boxes and carts thanks to experience with the speed of restaurant work.

Next semester, I will start my term as the Interfraternity Council's (IFC) Vice President of Community & Belonging, in which I will coordinate with every fraternity to ensure they have all planned necessary trainings related to hazing and violence prevention.

Additionally, I was offered a special invitation to join the Order of Omega this semester but unfortunately declined due to the amount of work I believed it would add on top of my academics.
